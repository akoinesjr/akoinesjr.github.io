Michael Garfield (http://michaelgarfield.blogspot.com) uses his site to share his ideas and artwork. It's in a blog format where he shares his most recent post on the main page. The dark color scheme works great and the blue text adds emphasis and energy to the site.



Ars Technica is a tech news site that manages to clearly display summary and headline information about a wide variety of stories in a clear fashion, with creative font schemes used to bring some life to the site.

The Economist displays a variety of articles just like Ars Technica, but does it with a cleaner, more formal style that helps its writers establish themselves as a serious, intelligent authority.
